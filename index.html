HOW TO PLAY

DESKTOP CONTROLS

  • W / S: Pitch Forward / Backward
  • A / D: Roll Left / Right
  • SPACE / SHIFT: Thrust Up / Down
  • Q / E: Yaw (Rotate) Left / Right
  • R: Reset Drone

MOBILE CONTROLS

  • Left Joystick: Drag Up/Down for Thrust (Altitude), Left/Right to Rotate (Yaw).
  • Right Joystick: Drag to tilt Forward, Backward, Left, or Right to move.
  • Note: Letting go of the left joystick will automatically trigger Auto-Hover.
